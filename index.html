Fátima, Batalha, Óbidos & Nazaré

8-10h • Premium Full-Day from Lisbon

Embark on a unique journey combining spirituality, history and unforgettable landscapes in the heart of Portugal. We begin in Fátima, one of the world's most important pilgrimage sites, where tranquillity and devotion create a truly special atmosphere. We then head to Batalha, where the impressive Monastery of Santa Maria da Vitória reveals centuries of history and stunning Gothic architecture. The journey continues to Nazaré, a fishing village full of character, known for its traditions and magnificent views over the Atlantic. We conclude in Óbidos, a charming medieval village surrounded by walls, where narrow streets and whitewashed houses invite a peaceful stroll back in time. Every detail is tailored to your interests and pace, providing an authentic, enriching and truly memorable experience.

Arrábida & Setúbal

6-7h • Premium Day Experience from Lisbon

Escape the city and discover one of Portugal's best-kept secrets. This tour blends world-class wines, hidden beaches, mystical caves and authentic coastal flavours into a perfect day south of Lisbon. We start with a wine tasting at a renowned local estate, then descend to the breathtaking Portinho da Arrábida for a leisurely lunch by the turquoise sea. We explore the Lapa de Santa Margarida — a striking natural cave steeped in legend — before hiking through the wild beauty of Serra da Arrábida Natural Park. The day ends in Setúbal, where a tasting of the region's famous fried cuttlefish is the perfect finale. An unforgettable blend of nature, culture and gastronomy.
